Italian Spaghetti


Ingredients:
  • 8 ounces (225 grams) of spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons of high-quality olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, finely minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Finely grated Parmesan cheese, preferably freshly grated
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnishing

/
Instructions:
  1. 1, Begin by preparing the spaghetti according to the instructions provided on the package. Once cooked, drain the spaghetti and set it aside for later use.
  2. 2, In a generously sized skillet, heat a measured quantity of high-quality olive oil over a medium heat setting. When the oil has warmed, introduce the finely minced garlic to the skillet and proceed to saut√© it until it emits a fragrant aroma.
  3. 3, Carefully add the crushed tomatoes to the skillet, taking care to maintain a controlled cooking environment. To enhance the flavors, incorporate the dried basil and dried oregano into the mixture. As per your culinary preferences, season the amalgamation with a calculated balance of salt and freshly ground black pepper. Allow the sauce to gently simmer for a duration of 10 to 15 minutes, fostering an environment in which the flavors meld harmoniously.
  4. 4, Elegantly incorporate the pre-cooked spaghetti into the skillet, ensuring each strand is gracefully coated with the savory sauce. Employ a skillful tossing technique to achieve an optimal integration of ingredients.
  5. 5, As you approach the final stages of preparation, transfer the Italian spaghetti to an exquisite serving dish, deserving of this culinary masterpiece. In a manner reflective of culinary finesse, garnish the dish with a delicate sprinkling of finely grated Parmesan cheese, preferably fresh. To elevate the presentation further, artfully place fresh basil leaves atop the ensemble, creating a visual and olfactory delight.
  6. 6, Present the Italian spaghetti with a sense of pride, knowing that your culinary endeavors have resulted in a dish that embodies the essence of Italian cuisine. Savor each bite, allowing the symphony of flavors to dance upon your palate.
