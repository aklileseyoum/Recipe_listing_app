5 Bean Chilli Stew


Ingredients:
  • 1 cup black beans
  • 1 cup kidney beans
  • 1 cup pinto beans
  • 1 cup cannellini beans
  • 1 cup chickpeas
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 bell peppers, finely diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • High-quality olive oil
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, finely chopped fresh cilantro

/
Instructions:
  1. 1, In a substantial pot, heat a moderate amount of high-quality olive oil over medium heat. Add the finely chopped onion and diced bell peppers, and saut√© them until they have softened.
  2. 2, Introduce the minced garlic, chili powder, ground cumin, and paprika to the pot. Stir the mixture and let it cook for approximately 1 minute, until the aromas are released.
  3. 3, Incorporate the contents of the can of diced tomatoes, including their juices, and pour in the vegetable broth. Thoroughly combine the ingredients to ensure they are well integrated.
  4. 4, Introduce the black beans, kidney beans, pinto beans, cannellini beans, and chickpeas into the pot. Gently blend the assortment until they are uniformly distributed.
  5. 5, Season the mixture with salt and freshly ground black pepper, adjusting the quantities to your personal taste preferences. Allow the stew to reach a gentle simmer.
  6. 6, Lower the heat to a gentle setting, place the lid on the pot, and allow the stew to cook for approximately 20-25 minutes. This time frame will allow the flavors to meld harmoniously.
  7. 7, Taste the stew and make any necessary adjustments to the seasoning.
  8. 8, Serve the 5 Bean Chilli Stew while it is still hot. For an added touch, consider garnishing the stew with optional toppings such as shredded cheese, a dollop of sour cream, or a sprinkling of finely chopped fresh cilantro.
